# Frame 13E - Final Together Line

## Speaker
Together

## Director Notes
Voice actors: Alice and Eugene together.
Performance: two English aristocrats, intimate and resolved. Their voices should sound like a married couple who have survived war, grief, and terror together.
Pacing: clean, solemn, tightly timed. No melodrama; the oath matters more if it is controlled.

Scene emotion: married resolve, dangerous calm, not a shout

Can be delivered in unison or as two overlapped takes; Alice slightly warmer, Eugene lower and harder.

## Voiceover Text
God help anything that tries to stop us.
